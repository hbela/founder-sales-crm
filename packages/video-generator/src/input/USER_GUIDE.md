# Founder Sales CRM

A short product demo of Founder Sales CRM, the outreach-first sales pipeline built for founders.

## Homepage
<!-- route: / -->
Manage founder sales contacts in one place. Track prospects through a simple sales pipeline. Run outreach campaigns for different products. Send and preview reusable email templates. Remember follow-ups and see sales progress.

## Dashboard
<!-- route: /dashboard -->
The dashboard gives you a clear overview of your pipeline at a glance: contacts, emails sent, replies, meetings booked, and customers won, plus follow-ups due today.

## Contacts
<!-- route: /contacts -->
The contacts list is your single source of truth for every lead. Search and filter by status or company, then open any contact to see their full history.

## Pipeline
<!-- route: /pipeline -->
The pipeline visualizes your deals as they move from new lead to customer. Spot bottlenecks at a glance and keep every deal moving forward.

## Campaigns
<!-- route: /campaigns -->
Campaigns organize your outreach by product and target market. Set a window, track status from draft to completed, and measure what works.

## Templates
<!-- route: /templates -->
Templates let you write reusable emails with variables like first name and company. Preview exactly how a message looks before it goes out.

## Outreach
<!-- route: /outreach -->
The outreach queue is where your emails get sent. Queue one contact or hundreds, schedule them, and watch each move from pending to sent.

## Follow-ups
<!-- route: /followups -->
Follow-ups make sure nothing slips through the cracks. See tasks due today, add notes, and mark them complete as you turn replies into meetings.

## Products
<!-- route: /products -->
Products are the things you sell. Define each with a name, description, and website, then attach contacts, campaigns, and templates to it.
